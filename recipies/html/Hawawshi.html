Hawawshi

Brief introduction:

Hawawshi is a popular Egyptian street food that consists of a pita bread stuffed with spiced minced meat and onions. The dish is then baked or grilled until the bread is crispy and the meat is cooked through. Hawawshi is often served with a side of pickles and tahini sauce.

The origins of Hawawshi are unclear, but it is believed to have originated in Egypt and has been a popular street food for many years. It is now enjoyed throughout the Middle East and has even gained popularity in other parts of the world. Hawawshi is a delicious and filling meal that is perfect for a quick lunch or snack on the go.

Ingredients

  • Pita bread (4)
  • Minced meat (500g beef or lamb)
  • Onions (2)
  • Salt (1 tsp)
  • Black pepper (1 tsp)
  • Cumin (1 tsp)
  • Paprika (1 tsp)
  • Olive oil(1 tbsp)

Preparing Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix together the minced meat, finely chopped onions, salt, black pepper, cumin, paprika, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  3. Cut the pita bread in half and stuff each half with the meat mixture.
  4. Press the edges of the bread together to seal the meat inside.
  5. Place the stuffed bread on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the bread is crispy and the meat is cooked through.
  6. Serve hot with a side of pickles and tahini sauce. Enjoy!
